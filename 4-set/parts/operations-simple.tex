  \begin{theorem}[DeMorgan's Law (Theorem $7.4\; (15)$)]
    Let $X$ denote a set, and $A, B \subseteq X$.

    \[
      X \setminus (A \cup B) = (X \setminus A) \cap (X \setminus B)
    \]
  \end{theorem}
